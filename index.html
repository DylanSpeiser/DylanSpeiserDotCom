Dylan Speiser


Hi! I'm a Computer Engineering student at the University of Maryland, College Park. I want to make the world a better place by doing what I love, and for me that's engineering, acting, and filmmaking.

Here's what I've been working on:

6502 on PCBs

To teach myself how to design and assemble custom PCBs, I made the same 6502 kit I assembled back in 2020 as a PCB. After ordering it, assembling it, and iterating through some challenges, it works!

Model Playhouse

To get better at designing for and operating laser cutters, I designed a 1:500 scale model of the theater at the summer camp I grew up at. It even has all the benches and poles on the porches!

UMD Class Checker

I wanted an easy way to keep track of the classes I wanted to register for as they began to fill up. So, I created this web app that uses the official Testudo API to show you how full your classes are.

Java 6502 Emulator

Back in 2021, I was unsatisfied by the current selection of emulators for the 6502 computer kit I had just put together. So, I decided to make my own in Java. And, of course, it's open source.

This Website!

I've always wanted a personal home page. And wow! Now I have one! I coded the whole thing with HTML, CSS, and a sprinkle of Bootstrap.

Mystery Project...?

I have a couple of cool projects in the works that aren't done yet. But, as soon as they are, I'll post them here!
