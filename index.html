Project Phoenix: a Dylan Rewind Story

"Project Phoenix: a Dylan Rewind Story" was my Senior ode to College Park, and the growth and friendships I built there. It's a metaphorical journey through the crazy experiment called "college", and the personal growth it catalyzes. You can watch Project Phoenix here on YouTube!
Project Phoenix took over a year and a half to produce, and the work of over 40 cast and crew members. Learn more about the production of Project Phoenix at dylanrewind.com/projectphoenix

REST API Tutorials & WebUI for BMD Cameras

For my Summer 2024 internship with Blackmagic Design, I created a series of tutorials that explains how to use the built-in REST API for controlling cameras and HyperDecks over the network. The finale of this tutorial is a fully custom, open-source web app that synchronizes with the camera in real-time. This project allowed me to exercise and improve my skills in web design (specifically JavaScript) and computer networking. It's also a great way to show how technology can empower creative tools and workflows.
